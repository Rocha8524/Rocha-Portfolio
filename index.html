About

My name is Alexander Rocha. I am 30 years old and I was born and raised in the state of New Jersey. I graduated from Kean University majoring in history and secondary education. I have taught middle and high school social studies in London, UK and in New Jersey. I am currently a high school social studies teacher and an aspiring web developer.

My goal for this year is to complete my web developent bootcamp course and use my newly developed skiils to help middle school and high school students. Not only do I want to teach my students how to code, but I also want to develop new software programs and apps that will enhance student learning and support teachers in the classroom.
